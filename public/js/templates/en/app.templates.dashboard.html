new report

To insert a report you must first insert the address of your darkroom, from the drop-down menu automatically proposed

Then select the type of report you want to do from the proposed

Enter a short description in the description field (Ex: my private darkroom) and then a more detailed description in the notes field

IMPORTANT The inclusion of field notes is very important because this is then derived tags (keywords) of your report

The tags will be used by the search engine darkroom to perform searches more accurate

Therefore it is very important to include a detailed description of his own darkroom (Ex: Darkroom private garage 35mm medium format and large format black and white)

IMPORTANT Remember, you can manually move the icon to make a selection of the finest location on the map

At this point, only if you want, you can enter your email address and your website to make sure that other users can contact you if you want to share your space with other photographers

IMPORTANT The e-mail and the website will be displayed in search results if you'll be to include them in this phase

search engine

    The search engine is a "public" feature that does not require access to the private area

    Through this engine you can search for darkrooms (or other types of reports) that best meet your search criteria

    These criteria are :

    • Where? only mandatory field, the "spatial" starting point of research
    • Within? optional, enlarges (or shrinks) spatial research of reports
    • What? optional, allows you to enter keywords (tags) to improve research
    • Which? optional, allows filtering by reports type

    IMPORTANT The results are always shown on maps
